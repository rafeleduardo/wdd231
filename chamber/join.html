Non-Profit Membership

No Fee

For non-profit organizations. Includes a directory listing and access to networking events.

  • Basic directory listing
  • Access to monthly networking events
  • Chamber newsletter subscription

Bronze Membership

$100 / year

Includes all NP benefits plus a featured spot on the homepage twice a year.

  • All Non-Profit benefits, plus:
  • Enhanced directory listing
  • Company logo in the directory
  • 2 spotlight features on the home page per year

Silver Membership

$250 / year

Includes all Bronze benefits plus social media promotion and event discounts.

  • All Bronze benefits, plus:
  • Priority placement in directory search results
  • 2 social media promotions per year
  • 10% discount on event sponsorships and tickets

Gold Membership

$500 / year

Includes all Silver benefits plus a banner ad on the site and top placement in the directory.

  • All Silver benefits, plus:
  • Top-tier placement in the directory
  • Banner ad on the directory page for 6 months
  • Company name and logo featured in all newsletters
  • 25% discount on event sponsorships and tickets
