Site Plan: Sabor de Casa

1. Site Name

Sabor de Casa

Domain: rafeleduardo.github.io/wdd231/final

"Sabor de Casa" (Taste of Home) captures the essence of family cooking with Latin warmth while remaining universally understood. The name evokes the authentic flavors, love, and memories that come from homemade meals across cultures. It's memorable, warm, and clearly communicates the site's mission of preserving family recipes and culinary traditions from around the world.

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of Sabor de Casa is to preserve and share family recipes from around the world, keeping alive the flavors, techniques, and precious stories that might otherwise be lost. The site provides an interactive cooking experience with cultural context and a space for users to contribute their own family food memories and traditions.

3. Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A young adult whose grandmother recently passed away visits the site to find her traditional empanada recipe. They browse the recipe shelf, read the story about Sunday family gatherings, and use the cooking view to prepare the dish step-by-step, feeling connected to their heritage.
  • Scenario 2: A home cook exploring international cuisines discovers the site and finds an authentic Italian pasta recipe with a story about the family's immigration journey. After successfully making the dish, they visit the memory corner to share their experience and thank the contributor for preserving this culinary tradition.

4. Color Schema

The color palette evokes warmth, tradition, and the comfort of home cooking, inspired by terracotta pottery, fresh herbs, and rustic kitchen environments. All colors have been verified for WCAG AA accessibility compliance.

  • Primary/Accent: A rich terracotta (#C1502E) for headings, buttons, and emphasis—reminiscent of clay cookware and warmth.
  • Background: A soft cream (#F8F5F0) that creates a warm, inviting canvas like aged recipe cards.
  • Secondary Accent: A deep olive green (#2C3E2E) for secondary elements, evoking fresh herbs and tradition.
  • Text: A dark chocolate brown (#3D2818) for excellent readability and warmth.
  • Card Background: Pure white (#FFFFFF) to make recipe photos and content pop beautifully.

5. Typography

  • Headings: Merriweather (from Google Fonts). A traditional serif font that conveys warmth, reliability, and timeless quality—perfect for recipe titles and section headings that honor family heritage.
  • Body Text: Open Sans (from Google Fonts). A highly legible, friendly sans-serif ideal for recipe instructions, ingredient lists, and storytelling. Its clarity ensures recipes are easy to follow while cooking.
